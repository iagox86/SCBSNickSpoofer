Minimized View

Minimize the Nick Spoofer by clicking the Minimize button. The Nick Spoofer will now be hidden from the taskbar and minimized directly to your system tray, the portion of icons to the left of your clock. You will notice the Nick Spoofer icon. Right-click it to get a list of options, Restore, Enter Nickname... and Exit, each of which serves a specific purpose.

  • Restore - Restores the Nick Spoofer to its pre-minimization status.
  • Enter Nickname... - Displays a small dialog box to enter your nickname that you wish to spoof as.
  • Exit - Exits the Nick Spoofer.
